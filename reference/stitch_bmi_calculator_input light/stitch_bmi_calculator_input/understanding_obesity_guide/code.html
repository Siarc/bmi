What is Obesity?

Obesity is a complex, chronic disease involving an excessive amount of body fat. It's more than just a cosmetic concern; it's a medical problem that increases the risk of other diseases.

Clinical Definition

BMI ≥ 30.0

Under Normal Overweight Obese
